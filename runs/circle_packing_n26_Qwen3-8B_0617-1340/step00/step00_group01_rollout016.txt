    Constraint to ensure circle is within the square.

    return x - radius >= 0 and y - radius >= 0 and x + radius <= square_size and y + radius <= square_size

    Constraint to ensure circles do not overlap.

    dx = center1[0] - center2[0]
    dy = center1[1] - center2[1]
    dist = np.sqrt(dx ** 2 + dy ** 2)
